How Can We Help You?

Getting Started

Welcome to TakaHub! Here's how to get started with our platform:

For Buyers

  • Create a free account
  • Browse waste materials and upcycled products
  • Add items to your cart
  • Complete checkout and payment
  • Track your orders and deliveries

For Sellers (Waste Sellers & Artisans)

  • Register with your specific role
  • Complete your profile information
  • List your materials or products
  • Manage your listings and orders
  • Communicate with buyers and drivers

For Delivery Drivers

  • Register as a driver
  • Update your profile and availability
  • Accept delivery assignments
  • Update delivery status
  • Manage your schedule and earnings

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click on the "Account" button in the navigation bar and select "Sign Up". Fill in your details and choose your role (Buyer, Artisan, Waste Seller, or Driver).
How do I list waste materials for sale?
Register as a Waste Seller, go to your profile, and click "List Waste". Fill in the material details, price, quantity, and location.
How do artisans list upcycled products?
Register as an Artisan, access your dashboard, and click "Add New Product". Upload images, set pricing, and provide product descriptions.
How does the delivery system work?
When you place an order, our system assigns a driver. You can track the delivery status in real-time through your order details.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept cash on delivery and MPESA payment gateway for secure online payments.
How do I contact customer support?
Use the floating communication widget on any page, visit our Contact page, or reach us via WhatsApp or email.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
Orders can be cancelled if they haven't been packed yet. Contact us immediately if you need to make changes.
How do I leave a review?
After your order is delivered, go to your order details and click "Write Review" to rate products and drivers.
