Our Mission

At TakaHub, we aim to bridge the gap between waste producers and creative artisans. We empower local communities by transforming discarded materials into valuable products, fostering sustainability and promoting a circular economy.

How It Works

1. Waste Generation

Waste sellers (households, businesses, institutions) generate and list recyclable materials on our platform.

2. Sorting & Listing

Materials are categorized and made available for purchase by artisans and buyers.

3. Delivery

Drivers collect materials and deliver them to artisans or buyers.

4. Upcycling

Artisans transform waste into unique products for resale.

Why Choose Us?

  • Eco-Friendly: We reduce landfill waste through sustainable practices.
  • Empower Artisans: We provide materials for artisans to create and sell.
  • Community Impact: Supporting local businesses and job creation.
  • Efficient Logistics: Seamless delivery from trash points to artisans.
